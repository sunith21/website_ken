Our Process

Three Simple Steps

1

Getting to Know You

We begin by understanding your income, commitments and future plans. Every client is different, so we listen before discussing any options.

2

Exploring Your Options

We research products available to us and explain how they work — including costs, features and potential risks — so you understand the implications before making any decision.

3

Recommendation & Ongoing Support

If we recommend a solution, it is because we believe it is suitable for your circumstances. We guide you through the application process and remain available to answer questions.

We are committed to supporting vulnerable customers and will adapt our communication and approach where appropriate.
