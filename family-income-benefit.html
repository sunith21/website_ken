Ongoing Financial Support for Your Family

Family Income Benefit provides a regular income to your loved ones if you pass away during the policy term. We help you understand how it works and whether it may be suitable for your needs.

What We Help With

Family Income Benefit is designed to provide your family with a steady income to help manage everyday living costs.

Regular Income for Your Family

Instead of a lump sum, this cover pays a monthly or annual income to your beneficiaries.

Supporting Everyday Expenses

The income can help with mortgage payments, bills, childcare and general living costs over time.

Understanding How It Works

We explain how the policy term, payout period and income level are structured.

Choosing Suitable Cover

We help you decide the level of income that may be appropriate based on your family's needs.

What to Expect

We provide clear and straightforward advice to help you understand your protection options.

  • Understanding your personal and family situation
  • Explaining how the cover works
  • Guidance on selecting appropriate protection
  • Ongoing support as your needs change
